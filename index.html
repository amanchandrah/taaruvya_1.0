How to Order

How Your Batch Comes to Life

1

Batch Booking

Orders are placed one week in advance. We strictly craft micro-batches of 20 to 60 total orders weekly — once the week is full, the churn closes.

2

Confirmation & Invoice

Upon booking, an advance payment invoice is generated. Production begins immediately on payment confirmation — coffee, cardamom and fruit sourced fresh for your batch.

3

Delivery / Pickup

Your order is safely prepared, sealed and fulfilled on schedule — collected at the atelier or delivered chilled to your door.

✦

Cancellation, Gracefully Handled

Plans change — we understand. Cancellations are fully permitted up to 4 days before your scheduled fulfilment date, with the advance returned in full. Within 4 days, production is already underway and orders are final.
